About Us

Books for the Field of Medicine, with focus on acute care treatment and disease management as practiced in and Family Practice, Urgent Care and Emergency Medicine - with special focus on the professions of Nurse Practitioner and Physician Assistant.
The author Donald Correll, M.D. is a former Emergency Department Medical Director of Jackson-Madison County General Hospital (Tennessee), which treats 100,000 acute care patients annually.
