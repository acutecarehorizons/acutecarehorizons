A compendium of acute care protocols, practice and disease management guidelines in 2 book versions created for the Nurse Practitioner and Physician Assistant. Concise, fast-reading topics for family practice, urgent care, and emergency medicine. These books provide differential diagnosis and practice guidance in a bulleted outline format. They are a management tool for patient care and to improve risk.

NOTE: The practice guides in the Practice Guide books are similar in content to the protocols in the Acute Care Protocols books. However, the Acute Care Protocols and Disease Management books contain  additional pages on disease management topics.
