Protocols and Guidelines eBooks

A compendium of acute care protocols and disease management guides created for the Physician Assistant and Nurse Practitioner. Over 150 concise, fast-reading protocols for family practice, urgent care, and emergency medicine. Covers cardiovascular, respiratory, endocrine, toxicology, neurology, musculoskeletal, GI, GU disorders, electrolyte and acid/base disturbances, HEENT, trauma, pediatrics, geriatrics, OB/GYN, infectious disease, dermatology, hematology, psychiatric and social, environmental, and disease management, among others. The book provides differential diagnosis and practice guidance in a bulleted outline format. This book has evidenced-based care and best practices and is a risk management tool. The author Donald Correll, M.D. has served over 15 years as the Emergency Department Medical Director of Jackson-Madison County General Hospital (Tennessee), which treats 85,000 patients annually.
